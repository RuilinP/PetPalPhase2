LEMUR's Story

Lemur is a very sweet and cuddly boy who is named for his large, round eyes and funny face that make him look like he is part lemur monkey! Lemur has lived most of his life outdoors as a semi-feral cat, but has since warmed up to humans and indoor living and is now looking for a human and home of his very own.

Lemur is a funny character who chases his own tail, somersaults while he is playing, and loves to be pet so much he drools a bit. He is always ready for attention from his humans and takes any opportunity to jump up beside you on the couch or bed to settle in for an endless pet session - he would make a great TV watching buddy!

While he does love attention, Lemur is not a high maintenance or clingy cat. He entertains himself by galloping up and down the hallway, leaping up cat trees, and playing with his favourite Yeowww catnip toys. He has a lot of energy for an older gentleman and would love an active playmate to chase around and get chased by - he is very good and confident with other cats. He also has perfect litterbox habits.

Lemur has early-stage kidney disease, which is very common in cats as they age. It is easily managed by a special diet, which he loves, and twice weekly sub-Q injections, which help keep him hydrated. He is okay with receiving his subQs as long as there are lots of pets and treats involved.

Having grown up on the streets, Lemur is shy around strangers and would need an adjustment period to get used to a new space with new people. After earning his trust, he would keep any human lucky enough to choose him thoroughly entertained with his quirky personality. Lemur is just waiting for his retirement home where he can receive lots of attention from his very own human!
